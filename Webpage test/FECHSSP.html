Shelina Paradis, ARNP-BC

M.N.S, GNP-C

Shelina has been a nurse practitioner since 2011, specializing in Gerontology and Cardiovascular nursing through the American Nurses Credentialing Center. Shelina has dedicated her entire career to the care of Brevard County’s Aging and Elderly population for the last 20 years. She was a successful operations partner from 1997-2001 in two Assisted Living Facilities. She left custodial care to pursue professional nursing, which she spent working in every level of care and nearly every setting. This has given her a rich background of facilitating communication and interactions with her peers at all levels.

Shelina founded Florida East Coast Health Services, LLC in 2014 and began working in collaboration with many local physicians and specialists. She has three main objectives for her career: to continue expanding Florida East Coast Health Services, LLC; to expand elder-specific health care in ALFs and residential homes throughout the region; and to be active in representing and marketing in the healthcare industry.

Shelina is married and has four children who are now 21 to 32 years old.
